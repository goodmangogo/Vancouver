    What Films Did You Miss?

    Although Toronto is often described as "the film capital" of Canada, Vancouver could very well end up usurping that title. Over 300 major productions have been shot there during the last three decades, and the city's popularity with movie producers shows no sign of abating. The latest production scheduled to shoot there: "The Interview", Vancouver native Seth Rogen's much anticipated follow up to his smash hit "This Is The End".
    -Michalea Ludwig(BC MAG.)
