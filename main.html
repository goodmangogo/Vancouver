Everywhere Else But Vancouver

Vancouver is North America's Third Biggest Film City. It is often called Hollywood North from
the movie people. Over 16 North American Movies and TV shows were shot and were planned to be shot
during 2015(Vancity Buzz, March) including DeadPool, Fear of the Walking Dead, The Flash, and Star Trek 3
and many more. These movies have different genres but these different movies have the
settings everywhere but Vancouver.

Weird Fact

Universities in Vancouver Plays Everywhere but Vancouver.
UBC always plays an University, BCIT, as a Government Facility,
and SFU as an Evil Corporation.

Why HollyWood North?

The filming industry Loves Vancouver bacause...

  • Tax Credits
  • Vancouver is Multicultural, Vancouver can film India, New York, Seattle, and Eastern Europe
    all within a 15-minute drive of each other
  • Location: Vancouver is only 1725 km away from Holly Wood!
  • Weather: Vancouver is mostly cloudy, which makes it much more easier for film editors to add direct light
  • Experience: Vancouver has been loved by Film Crews by the reasons above. Many Filmcrews have experiences fliming in Vancouver
